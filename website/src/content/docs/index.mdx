## Quick Start

1. **Download** the installer for your platform from the [Installation](/argus/installation/) page
2. **Launch** Argus — it starts as a tray app and opens `http://localhost:3000`
3. **Configure** your AI agent to send telemetry — see [Agent Setup](/argus/setup-guide/)
4. **Monitor** costs, tokens, sessions, and more in real-time
